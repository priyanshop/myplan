Workout & Diet Plan

Workout Plan - 6 Days a Week

  • Day 1 - Chest & Triceps (Push)
  • Incline Barbell Bench Press | 4x6-8 | RPE 8
  • Flat Dumbbell Press | 3x8-10 | RPE 8
  • Chest Dips | 3x8-10 | RPE 8
  • Cable Chest Fly (Incline) | 3x10-12 | RPE 7
  • Overhead Dumbbell Tricep Extension | 3x10-12 | RPE 7
  • Tricep Pushdowns | 3x10-12 | RPE 7
  • Day 2 - Back & Biceps (Pull)
  • Deadlifts | 4x5-6 | RPE 9
  • Pull-Ups (Weighted if possible) | 3x6-8 | RPE 8
  • Bent-Over Rows (Barbell) | 4x8-10 | RPE 8
  • Lat Pulldowns | 3x10-12 | RPE 7
  • Dumbbell Bicep Curls | 3x10-12 | RPE 7
  • Cable Rope Face Pulls | 3x10-12 | RPE 7
  • Day 3 - Legs & Abs
  • Barbell Squats | 4x6-8 | RPE 8
  • Romanian Deadlifts | 3x8-10 | RPE 8
  • Walking Lunges (Dumbbell/Barbell) | 3x10-12 (each leg) | RPE 8
  • Leg Press | 3x8-10 | RPE 8
  • Hanging Leg Raises | 3x12-15 | RPE 7
  • Plank (Weighted if possible) | 3 sets | 60 seconds
  • Day 4 - Shoulders & Arms
  • Seated Dumbbell Shoulder Press | 4x6-8 | RPE 8
  • Lateral Raises | 3x12-15 | RPE 7
  • Arnold Press | 3x8-10 | RPE 8
  • Barbell Bicep Curls | 3x8-10 | RPE 8
  • Preacher Curls | 3x10-12 | RPE 7
  • Cable Tricep Extensions | 3x10-12 | RPE 7
  • Day 5 - Full Body (Conditioning)
  • Barbell Clean & Press | 4x5-6 | RPE 9
  • Kettlebell Swings | 3x12-15 | RPE 8
  • Battle Ropes (30 seconds) | 3 sets | Max Effort
  • Farmer's Carry (Heavy Dumbbells) | 3 sets | 40-50 meters
  • Burpees | 3x12-15
  • Day 6 - Glutes, Hamstrings & Core
  • Hip Thrusts | 4x8-10 | RPE 8
  • Glute Kickbacks | 3x10-12 | RPE 7
  • Cable Pull-Throughs | 3x10-12 | RPE 7
  • Hamstring Curls | 3x8-10 | RPE 8
  • Cable Woodchoppers | 3x12 reps (each side)
  • Day 7 - Rest or Active Recovery
  • Light walk, stretching, or yoga
